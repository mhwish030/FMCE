How much weight can I expect to lose?

In clinical studies of Tirzepatide, the FDA approved active ingredient in Mounjaro®, patients achieved 21.1% weight loss during a 36-week lead-in period and an additional 6.7% weight loss during a 52-week continued treatment period, for a total mean weight loss of 27.8% over 88 weeks. This equates to a weight loss of 55.6 pounds for a 200 pound person.

Losing significant weight over time has been shown to result in continued weight loss, reduced risk for weight regain, and successful long-term weight loss maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do Slender Strides consultations work?

To complete a Slender Strides consultation, all you need to do is answer our quick online medical intake questionnaire, and verify your identity by uploading a photo ID, such as a valid drivers license or state ID. Your answers are then reviewed by our medical team, who will notify you during your initial telehealth (audio-visual) appointment whether you are eligible for treatment, and which weight loss medication will work best for you.

Is GLP-1 medication FDA approved?

Semaglutide and Tirzepatide are FDA-Approved active ingredients used in Ozempic®, Wegovy® and Mounjaro®, that have helped thousands of people lose and maintain weight loss.

Medically approved patients are typically prescriped Semaglutide or Tirzepatide injections, which are slowly absorbed by the body over a weeks duration, to help curb your appetite and reduce cravings. The Semaglutide or Tirzepatide GLP-1 medication you may be prescribed is a generic compounded medication and is not associated with the pharmaceutical company Novo Nordisk™ or Eli Lilly™, or the brand-name medications Ozempic®, Wegovy® and Mounjaro®.

Is your weight loss program confidential?

Yes. Your medical data and order history can only be viewed by our medical team and prescribers. You can view our Privacy Policy for more information.

Do I need a prescription?

If you're eligible for treatment, our medical team will issue you a private prescription. You don't need an existing prescription to join the Slender Strides weight loss program.

Does your program include the prescription and medication?

Yes. Our weight loss program does not have any hidden fees. The telehealth consultation, prescription, medication and shipment are all included in your plan.

How often will I receive a shipment?

Your shipment will arrive promptly after our doctors review your monthly progress and issue the next prescription. You will always have medicine on hand to continue your weight loss journey.

Is packaging discreet?

Yes. We use plain packaging without any branding or identifying labels.

What is your 1:1 monthly telehealth visit for?

A monthly follow up telehealth appointment is necessary, to discuss with your clinician how your weight loss is progressing. Your clinician will determine if your prescribed GLP-1 medication such as Semaglutide (the active ingredient in Ozempic®, Wegovy®) or Tirzepatide (the active ingredient in Mounjaro®) needs to be adjusted to achieve your healthy weight loss goals.

What will appear on my bank statement?

ACH, Debit or Credit Card payments will show on your bank statement under the name Slender Strides.

Safety Information

Notify your provider immediately if you notice a lump or swelling in your neck, hoarseness, difficulty swallowing, or shortness of breath. These could be symptoms of thyroid cancer. Semaglutide or Tirzepatide is contraindicated if you or any of your family members have ever had medullary thyroid carcinoma (MTC), or if you suffer from an endocrine system condition called Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2 (MEN 2). The safety and efficacy of combining Semaglutide or Tirzepatide with other weight loss products have not been established. Sharing your complete medical history with your provider is crucial. Specifically, inform your provider if you have a history of: Type 1 or type 2 diabetes, Thyroid cancer, Pancreatitis, Kidney disease, and or Diabetic retinopathy.
